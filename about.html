About Infinite Fitness

5 Simple Steps on How the Program Works

Click here to sign up for a free trial!
1. Sign-up With Us
  • Start out by filling out our initial client consultation. It takes less than 5 minutes!
  • Pick which days you want to workout based on your schedule.
2. Perform Initial Monthly Testing
  • To get a foundation and starting point for your results, as well as so we can calculate where you will begin in our program we need to get an initial fitness test. This will allow us to determine appropriate workouts for you to start so you can see results ASAP.
3. Begin Your Workouts and Experience Success!
  • After we receive initial testing, you will get your results chart and your first monthly workout schedule.
  • Based on the days you selected and your monthly workout schedule you will receive an email with a link to your workout on the specific morning of your workout.
4. Recieve Your Results
  • Each month completed you will retest and get an updated version of your results.
  • We also look over your results to determine if a different level or sublevel would be right for you.
5. Progress Through Our Level System
  • Each level and sublevel of our program is unique with no workout ever the same!
  • Each level consists of 3 sublevels each with a different training goals if endurance, strength, and hypertrophy.
  • Each sublevel is 1 month allowing you to cycle through these microcycles as you progress up the levels.
