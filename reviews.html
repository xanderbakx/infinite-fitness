Reviews

What do our clients think about Infinite Fitness?

Female, 26

PhD Graduate Student

Client Results Breakdown

“Before this client started the program she was mainly training cardiorespiratory endurance. In the first month of training with the addition of upper body and lower body muscular strength/fitness, there was a slight increase in weight due to the body converting some upper body fat into denser and leaner muscle. She has shown and continues to show significant improvements in cardiorespiratory fitness, core strength, lower body muscular endurance and upper body strength with continued training.”

What was your fitness lifestyle like before beginning Infinite Fitness?

Before Infinite Fitness, most of my workouts were walking either outside or on the treadmill 5 times a week and going to the sauna twice a week. It was a lot lower impact, so I was dedicated to more days a week than I needed with Infinite Fitness.

What inspired you to make a change and workout more/ be more motivated and determined?

Since I was doing less intense workouts, I had to do them every day in order to maintain my weight and not feel guilty about indulging in food. The repetition was getting boring, so I wanted to mix up my routine as well as cut back on days while getting healthier overall.

What was/is the best part about training with Infinite Fitness?

The trainers are awesome. They always have time to answer a quick text, so even though everything is done online (which is also super convenient since all my workouts are on my phone), I didn't lose the close interaction I would have received working one-on-one with a trainer. It's also nice that I don't physically have to go to the gym at all to work out.

How has Infinite Fitness made your fitness lifestyle better?

I've gotten stronger and more toned without even leaving my house. Other people started commenting on the amount of muscle I've gained even after the first month, so it's nice to know there's a noticeable difference that other people can tell as well since that was one of my goals. My other goal was to gain endurance, and cardio has gotten much easier for me. I've gone from walking 15-minute miles before Infinite Fitness to running 2 miles in 15 minutes after I started.
