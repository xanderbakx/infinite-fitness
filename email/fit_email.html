Thank you for your interest in Infinite Fitness! Our goal at Infinite Fitness is to provide an all inclusive, easily accessible, and easy to follow workout program that gives you personal training on your time and schedule.

We are excited to work with you and help track your progress as you work through our program in the comfort of your home. Each workout is unique and structured according to the fitness level of the individual. Each month is different and introduces new challenges to overcome and progress up the levels in our level system.

Our level system is broken down into 3 month mesocycles each month focusing on different aspects of training such as. . .

  • Cardiorespiratory and Muscular Endurance
  • Muscular Hypertrophy
  • Muscular Strength and Speed

We also incorporate flexibility and balance training into each month to increase functionality and to aid in preventing injury.
