Gymnsatics Abs (Advanced)

Thank you for your interest in Infinite Fitness! Our goal at Infinite Fitness is to provide an all inclusive, easily accessible, and easy to follow workout program that gives you personal training on your time and schedule.

We have created one of the toughest whole body core workout programs on the market! We can guarantee that you will see significant results in your core strength and physical results in your waist and abdominal region.

This extreme gymnastics based abdominal training is also based on Triphasic Training which breaks down and focuses on each type of muscular contraction each week:

  • Eccentric (week 1)
  • Isometric (week 2)
  • Concentric (week 3)
  • Triphasic Combination (week 4)

During this program you will workout 4 times per week in a variety of environments. You get to pick the days that you want to workout. Workouts should not last longer than 1 hour.

It is necessary that you have access to the following equipment. However not everything listed will be used every workout:

  • Hanging bar
  • Dip bar, Parallel bar, or Roman Chair
  • Ab Wheel
  • Exercise Ball
  • Treadmill
  • Bungees / Bands
  • Jump Rope
  • Open Field for sprinting (up to 40 yards)

We are excited for you to get the strong, good looking abs that you have been wanting and working on for that summer beach body!
